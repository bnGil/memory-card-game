How to play?

The player turns over any two cards (one at a time).
If they successfully match a pair the cards remains visible.
When the player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again (in the same position).
The trick is to remember which cards are where.
The player's wins the game after revealing all the cards.
Try to win the game as fast as you can and with as few wrong guesses as possible.

Enjoy and good luck!
